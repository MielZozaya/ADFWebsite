Exercise 2 : How My Relative's Behaviour Affects Me

Think carefully about problems that have been caused by your relative’s drinking or drug use. Think about your relative’s behaviour and how this affects you. Now describe these problems and how they affect you and your family.

Below are two examples to help you along in this exercise.  Point your cursor to the empty box below and "left click" on the mouse button, Type in the information relevant to your own situation in the boxes. When you have finished select the "submit" button.  This will automatically upload your responses and clear the space for you to type in more responses.  When you are through with this exercise, click on "next page" button at the bottom of this window, to continue with the programme.
