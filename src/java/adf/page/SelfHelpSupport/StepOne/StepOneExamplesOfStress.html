Behaviours That Can Lead to Stress

Living with your relative who is drinking or taking drugs can lead to many different forms of stress. Below are some examples of behaviours that can lead to stress. Of course, not all of them will apply to everyone.

  • "My relative can be threatening and violent."  
  • "My relative is restless at night."
  • "My relative is possessive and jealous.“
  • "We don’t go out very much as a family."
  • "My relative doesn’t join in family activities and occasions."
  • "I don’t trust him/her."
  • "My relative borrows and steals."
  • "My relative is foolish and embarrassing."
  • "My relative has damaged our property."
  • "We don’t talk as much as we used to."
  • "My relative can be irritable and moody."
  • "My relative spoils special occasions."
  • "My relative is manipulative and always lying."
  • "My relative is unpredictable and can go missing."
  • "My relative is lethargic and doesn’t look after him/herself."
