Goals of this step

Summary of Step One

Step One should have helped me to:

  • Think more about my situation and the problems that I might be having
  • Identify stresses and strains relevant to my situation and that of my family  
  • Identify any health problems that I have

It is possible that you may feel upset and overwhelmed after looking at Step One. It is okay to feel like this.  Other family members in similar circumstances, have told us they often feel like this. You may feel that the only solution to your situation is for your relative to stop drinking or taking drugs. Your relative will seek help when the time is right for them but in the meantime this programme can help you to deal with you and the problems that you are having.

Try to work through the rest of the programme in your own way and in your own time so it can be of help to you.

If you want, you could go over Step One again before you proceed to other steps in the programme.
