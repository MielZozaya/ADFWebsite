Strain Felt by Other Family/Network Members

Here are some experiences that family/network members have reported as occurring in their own situations. You may be able to identify with some of these comments:

  • "My/Our home life/social life is affected."
  • "There are harmful effects on the family as a whole."
  • "I am concerned about my relative’s health or performance."
  • "I/we have financial difficulties."
  • "I find my relative unpleasant or difficult to live with."
  • "There are major incidents and crises."
  • "I have poor health and/or specific physical symptoms."
  • "I feel: anxious/worried, helpless/despairing, low/depressed."
