Self Help Intervention

There are five steps to working through this programme.

  • Work through all the steps tackling one step at a time in the suggested order. (recommended)
  • Work through the steps in your own order.
  • Do a few steps at a time focusing on some sections of the programme, and going back over sections that you have done in the past. 

You will find several exercises are provided at each step.  These exercises are to help you think through the issues raised in each section, we strongly encourage you to do them as other family/network members that have used them previously have found them very useful.
