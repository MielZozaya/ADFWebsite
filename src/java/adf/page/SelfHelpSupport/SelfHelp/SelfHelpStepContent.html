Content of Each Step

This self-help programme is arranged in modules or steps. Each step with a particular aim and focus:

  • Step One
    How the problem affects me and my family:

    Looks at how the excessive alcohol or drug use of someone in your family is affecting you.

  • Step Two
    Getting Information:

    Gives you the information that will help you most. This information should increase your knowledge and address any fears and misunderstandings that you may have.

  • Step Three
    Responding:

    Looks at how you deal with the situation. To look at advantages and disadvantages of how you currently respond and whether you could respond any differently.

  • Step Four
    Support:

    Looks at the support that you get from other people. To see if there are any new forms of support that could be more helpful to you.

  • Step Five
    Further Help:

    To see if there is any more help that you could get.
